ENDING ILLUSTRATIONS
====================

Drop an image here for any ending and it will automatically appear on that
ending's screen (replacing the drawn placeholder). No code changes needed.

Naming:  assets/endings/<ending-id>.png
         (.jpg and .webp also work — the game tries png, then jpg, then webp)

Recommended size: landscape, about 300 x 210 (any ~10:7 ratio works; it is
cover-fit into the frame). Match the game's flat ukiyo-e look if you like.

The ending ids (filename before the extension):

  legend.png            Legend of the Age            (great)
  unify.png             The Realm United             (great)
  satori.png            Satori                       (great)
  province_held.png     A Province Held              (good)
  revenge_done.png      Vengeance, and the Ash After (good)
  pilgrimage_done.png   The Pilgrimage Fulfilled     (good)
  fortune_made.png      A Fortune Made               (good)
  duty_done.png         Duty Discharged              (good)
  peace_sealed.png      The Peace Sealed             (good)
  contract_kept.png     The Contract Kept            (good)
  escape_done.png       A New Name, A New Life        (good)
  peoples_hero.png      The People's Champion        (good)
  survive_well.png      Weathered and Wiser          (good)
  survive_hard.png      A Hard Road Survived         (neutral)
  death_generic.png     The Road Ends Here           (bad)
  death_wild.png        Bones on the Mountain        (bad)
  death_hunted.png      Silenced                     (bad)

Example: to illustrate the Satori ending, save your image as
  assets/endings/satori.png
